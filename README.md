This project is a way to centralize many different components in life; whether it's game-ifying the menial tasks into a tabletop strategy, 
maintaining relationships and social connections, tracking general health and wellness, or keeping all the chaotic factors of the day-to-day in a manageable state,
the goal is to deploy this app to help people stay on top of it all. 

The app's "Council" is there to help suggest and guide action plans based on the input data through different views and dashboards.
  - The Battle Advisor maintains the to-do list, goals, projects, etc as opponents on a board. The user can then "fight" these opponents as tasks are completed
  - The Health Advisor provides an over view of mental health, fitness, nutrition and diet, and helps build and maintain goals and self-improvement
  - The Diplomacy Advisor helps maintain relationships with others by setting reminders to reach out, make plans, track birthdays, and relationship standings
  - The Economics Advisor tracks spending, budget, financial goals, etc to provide input on overall financial discipline and stability
  - The Romantics Advisor creates a space for partners to interact, play games together, connect, and set important dates to strengthen relationships
  - The Entertainment Advisor helps track hobbies, crafts, skills to learn, and how the user is feeling about their practices.
  - The Museum is a place to recognize acheivements made in the app by earning trophies, artifacts, medals, and art pieces.

All date-related components are forwarded to the interactive calendar to provide a solid overview of timelines and upcoming events
